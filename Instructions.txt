 
        Welcome to Hunt the Wumpus Game!
        ---------------------------------

        Instructions:
        1. You are an adventurer exploring a cave with hidden dangers.
        2. Your goal is to find the gold without encountering the wumpus or falling into pits.
        3. Move around using the arrow keys.
        4. You will sense a stench when the wumpus is nearby, and a breeze when a pit is nearby.
        5. Collect the gold to score points.
        6. Press 'H' to remove the walls of the world.
        7. Press 'Q' to quit the game.
        8. Good luck and have fun!

        Press any key to continue...
